NetWorking OccuPassion WorldWide

NOW is a weekly meeting forum for traders, professionals, executives, startups, investors, NGOs & hobbyist to support each others passions and goals. It is a Grass-root Good Governance project to recognise the 'Common Human Needs' to stay occupied with work, network with people and invite abundance for one self, family and society.

The focus of NOW is to create an opportunity for every individual to be occupied doing what one likes.

Action plan:

Individuals in neighbourhoods of every districts are invited to form a NOW chapter. It's scope includes all strata of society anyone with a passion, vision, mission, or a dream to share and achieve.

The Method:

Participants sit in a Circle. Each speak for 120 - 150 seconds on the topic "work I do and network support I need or offer." After introduction of each participants, they are guided to form Mutual Interest Group.

Agenda is evolved and consensus on action plan is arrived upon. Meeting concludes after voluntary feedback, Awards and photo shoot of participants.

Activities like daily Morning Walk or Cycling too can be inculcated among members. This would make the members to stay in a positive field, doing good to each other, prevent stress related diseases.

The chapter's core team can also connect with schools, colleges, government agencies and prominent people in their zone.

FB / WhatsApp social media may be maximized for connecting with residents of each zone in the city.

NOW is a step towards a Dynamic Upward Spiral Wellness, Peace, Prosperity and Safety for all.
